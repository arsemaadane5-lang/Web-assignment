Tibeb n’ T’ej

About the Event

Tibeb n’ T’ej is a unique Sip & Paint experience that blends the joy of painting with the warmth of Ethiopian culture.
Whether you’re coming with friends, on a date, or by yourself, Tibeb n’ T’ej is the perfect way to try something new, and leave with a painting you can hang.

Why Join?

  • Fun and Cultural twist
  • No experience needed
  • All materials included
  • Good for groups and friends
  • Connect and Socialize
